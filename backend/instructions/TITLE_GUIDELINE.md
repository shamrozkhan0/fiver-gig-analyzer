#### Title Guidline
- Length: ≤80 characters (Fiverr’s cap). (Tip: ideally ≤50 chars so it isn’t truncated on some displays.)
- Format: Start with “I will…” (as Fiverr’s UI demands) and follow with a specific service, target audience, and method/tool. For example, “I will design a modern minimalist logo for your startup in 24 hours”.
- Keywords: Include the main keyword naturally. E.g. if the gig is about SEO blog posts, use “SEO blog posts” or “blog posts that rank”. Keep it readable – no keyword stuffing.
- Specificity: Be concrete. Replace vague titles like “I will do SEO” (❌) with specific ones “I will write SEO blog posts that rank on Google” (✅).
Examples:
❌ “I will design a logo” – too generic.
✅ “I will design a modern minimalist logo for your startup in 24 hours”.
✅ “I will write SEO blog posts that rank on Google for SaaS companies”.
