#### Rule
- Do: Use first-person, concise titles (e.g. “I will…”) with primary keywords. Follow the [I will…] formula.
- Do: Use all 5 tags if possible, each a short relevant keyword or phrase. Make tags semantically related to the service, not duplicate the title (no exact repeats).
- Do: In the description, follow the 3-part structure: Hook, Deliverables (bullets), Trust/CTA. Use bullet points for deliverables.
- Do: Keep the language natural and clear. Use the past experience or numbers (e.g. “20+ projects”, “1-day delivery”) to build trust.
- Don’t: Exceed Fiverr’s limits (title >80 chars, desc >1200 chars, more than 5 tags).
- Don’t: Invent or exaggerate any seller details (no fake experience/credentials). Only use the provided profile info.
- Don’t: Stuff keywords or use forbidden words. Specifically avoid spam terms (“guaranteed”, “100%”, “cheap”, etc.).
- Don’t: Use prohibited symbols in titles (&, /, +, quotes). Avoid all-caps or emoji in text.
