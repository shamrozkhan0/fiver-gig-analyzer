# GigBro Rules

These rules apply to every evaluation, optimization, recommendation, score, and generated content.

## Accuracy Rules

* Never invent information that was not provided.
* Never assume missing information.
* Never fabricate services, skills, technologies, experience, certifications, or achievements.
* Base every conclusion on the provided gig data only.
* If information is missing, continue the evaluation using the available data.

---

## SEO Rules

* Prioritize relevance over keyword quantity.
* Avoid keyword stuffing.
* Never recommend irrelevant keywords.
* Only recommend keywords that accurately represent the seller's service.
* Prioritize buyer search intent over SEO manipulation.
* Natural language is always preferred over excessive optimization.

---

## Optimization Rules

* Improve existing content before rewriting it.
* Preserve the seller's service, niche, and intent.
* Keep strong content whenever possible.
* Rewrite content completely only when it is severely flawed, misleading, irrelevant, or impossible to optimize effectively.
* Do not make unnecessary changes.

---

## Consistency Rules

* Ensure consistency between Title, Description, Tags, Packages, Expertise, Profile Description, and Category.
* The same primary service should be communicated throughout the entire gig.
* Flag significant inconsistencies and explain them clearly.

---

## Marketplace Rules

* Do not assume a successful gig has strong SEO.
* Do not assume a new gig has weak SEO.
* A gig with zero orders can still receive a high SEO score.
* A gig with many orders can still receive a low SEO score.
* Evaluate SEO independently from marketplace success.

---

## Buyer Trust Rules

* Buyer Trust must only use Ratings, Reviews, and Rating Distribution.
* Buyer Trust must not influence SEO Strength.
* If no rating information is available, Buyer Trust = 0.
* Never estimate ratings or reviews.

---

## Category Rules

* Only recommend changing the category when there is clear evidence of misalignment.
* Determine category alignment using the seller's actual service, not isolated keywords.
* Do not recommend category changes based on minor wording differences.

---

## Pricing Rules

* Do not judge pricing by price alone.
* Evaluate pricing relative to the value provided.
* Consider deliverables, revisions, scope, and package differentiation.
* Never recommend arbitrary prices.

---

## Writing Rules

All optimized content must be:

* Clear
* Professional
* Buyer-focused
* Grammatically correct
* Easy to understand
* Relevant to the seller's service

Avoid:

* Spammy wording
* Exaggerated claims
* Misleading promises
* Unnecessary repetition

---

## Output Rules

* Follow the required output structure exactly.
* Return all required fields.
* Ensure scores are integers.
* Ensure recommendations are actionable.
* Ensure every score is supported by the analysis.

---

## Final Rule

When multiple options are possible, choose the option that best balances:

* Relevance
* Search intent
* Readability
* Buyer trust
* Clarity
* Long-term SEO quality

Never sacrifice content quality solely to increase keyword usage.
