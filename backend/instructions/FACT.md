#### Fiver FACT:
- Title Limit: Gig titles can be up to 80 characters. (Fiverr’s Pro FAQ explicitly states “up to 80 characters” for titles.)
- Tag Limit: Up to 5 tags per gig. (The official guide says “Add up to five tags”, and best practices urge using all 5.)
- Description Limit: Gig descriptions have a 1,200‑character maximum. (Fiverr’s help center: “Write a description of your Gig (up to 1,200 characters).”.)
- Required Style: Fiverr auto-inserts “I will…” at title start (implied by UI, so prompts should use this first-person style).
---
#### Best Practices
- Title Importance & Formula: The title is the most critical SEO signal. A proven formula is: I will [service] for [target] using [technique]. (E.g., “I will design a modern minimalist logo for your startup in 24 hours”.)
- Keyword Usage: Front-load top keywords in the title and the first 150 characters of the description (SwiftCopy notes that these carry the most weight). Use each keyword naturally.
- Tag Strategy: Tags should be highly relevant synonyms or related terms – not mere repeats of title words. Use Fiverr’s autocomplete for popular search terms. (E.g. for a logo gig, tags might include graphic design, branding, not just “logo design”.)
- Description Structure: A high-converting description follows a 3-block framework: Hook: 2–3 sentences framing the buyer’s problem and your solution (use a question or bold statement). Lead with the buyer’s situation (“Struggling to get your logo right?”) rather than your credentials.
Deliverables: A bullet list (3–5 items) of specific services/deliverables. Each bullet should clearly state a concrete deliverable (e.g. “3 unique logo concepts”), avoiding vague language.
Trust & CTA: 2–3 sentences of unique selling points (experience, credentials) and a clear call-to-action. (E.g., mention past successes and say “Message me with your project details…”.)
- Tone & Clarity: Write in concise, buyer-focused language (Fiverr suggests specificity). Avoid flowery or filler words. Use short paragraphs and lists for scan-ability.
- Avoid Penalties: Steer clear of “spammy” tactics. For instance, do not keyword-stuff or use trigger words like “guaranteed”, “100%”, “cheap”, etc.. Also avoid special symbols in the title (Fiverr explicitly says to “avoid & / +”).
